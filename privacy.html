Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Our Privacy Commitment

GetATSReady is built with privacy as our core principle. Unlike other resume checkers that upload your data to servers, we process everything 100% locally in your browser.

What Data We Collect

  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (page views, time spent, country). This data is anonymized.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for Google AdSense advertising and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
  • Your Resume: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your resume or job descriptions. All processing happens in your browser using JavaScript.

How We Use Your Information

  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Analyze traffic patterns and popular features

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements (Privacy Policy)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of cookies and analytics tracking
  • Request information about data we collect (though we don't store personal data)
  • Use ad blockers to prevent AdSense cookies

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your resume data, there's no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your personal information. All processing is done client-side in your browser.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
