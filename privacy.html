Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Our Privacy Commitment

GetATSReady is built with privacy as our core principle. Unlike other resume checkers that upload your data to servers, we process everything 100% locally in your browser.

What Data We Collect

  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (page views, time spent, country). This data is anonymized.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for Google AdSense advertising and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
  • Your Resume: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your resume or job descriptions. All processing happens in your browser using JavaScript.

User-Initiated Troubleshooting (Debug Log)

To assist with technical support, we provide a "Download Debug Log" feature. This feature is strictly user-initiated.

  • When you click "Download Debug Log", a small file is generated locally on your device.
  • This file contains technical metadata (browser type, error codes) and a truncated sample of extracted text (first 1,000 characters).
  • We do not receive this file automatically. You must choose to share it with us via email if you require support.

How We Use Your Information

  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Analyze traffic patterns and popular features

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your resume data, there's no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your personal information on our end. All processing is done client-side in your browser.
