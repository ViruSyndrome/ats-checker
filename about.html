About GetATSReady

Our Mission

We built GetATSReady to solve a simple problem: 75% of resumes never reach human eyes because they're filtered out by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

Most job seekers don't know why their applications are rejected. Is it formatting? Missing keywords? Poor structure? Our tool gives you instant, actionable feedback to fix these issues.

Why We're Different

  • 100% Private: Your resume never leaves your device. Everything is processed locally in your browser using JavaScript.
  • No Signup Required: We don't ask for your email, phone number, or payment information. Just upload and analyze.
  • Free Forever: Job hunting is stressful enough. We'll never charge you for basic ATS checking.
  • Intelligent Matching: Our semantic engine understands synonyms and related skills (e.g., "API" matches "REST", "GraphQL", "microservices").

How It Works

  1. Upload: Paste the job description and upload your resume (PDF or DOCX).
  2. Analyze: Our algorithm extracts keywords, checks structure, and measures impact metrics.
  3. Improve: Get a detailed score with specific recommendations to optimize your resume.
  4. Download: Generate a professional PDF report for your records.

Our Technology

GetATSReady uses advanced client-side processing:

  • PDF.js and Mammoth.js for document parsing
  • Custom keyword extraction with noise filtering
  • Bi-directional synonym matching across professional families
  • Multi-factor scoring: Structure (20%), Keywords (50%), Impact (30%)

Who We Help

  • Tech Professionals: Software engineers, data analysts, DevOps engineers, and IT specialists
  • Recent Graduates: Students entering the competitive job market for the first time
  • Career Changers: Professionals pivoting to new industries or roles
  • Experienced Professionals: Anyone applying to companies that use ATS systems
  • All Industries: Finance, marketing, healthcare, education, and more

Our Commitment

We're committed to keeping this tool free, private, and accessible to everyone. We earn revenue through ethical advertising (Google AdSense), which allows us to maintain and improve the service without charging users.
